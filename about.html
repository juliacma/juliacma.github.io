Julia Ma

Julia Ma is a designer at Miko McGinty, Inc. in Brooklyn, working on art books, exhibition graphics, and printed ephemera. She has worked with artists such as Cai Guo-Qiang and Do Ho Suh; museums like the Metropolitan Museum of Art, the American Museum of Natural History, and the Morgan Library & Museum; as well as publishers like DelMonico + D.A.P. and Yale University Press. Julia loves typefaces and letterforms, previously working for Tobias Frere-Jones and in the Jonathan Edwards College Printing Press at Yale University. She also collects zines, books, posters, keychains.
