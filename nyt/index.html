Julia Ma

New York Times 11.4.17

This project was an exercise in transforming the front page of the New York Times into a 12-page 8.5" x 11" booklet. Instead of redesigning the layout of texts and images, I decided to represent each article and section as a pair of windows, a pair of frames.

This was made in the fall of 2017. The original front page from that day may be found at the bottom of this site.
