Julia Ma

Diarrhea Planet

This poster was a redesign for a modeled client project, meaning my client was one of my classmates. He had brought in a flyer he was very attached to: one for a concert at the nearby dance club, Toad's. The flyer was very graphic: it had a drawing of a boy vomiting what looked like intestines out of his mouth. The flyer had gotten wet and the printed ink had an interesting watercolor effect, which my client really enjoyed. I have since lost my copy of the flyer.

This design was inspired by that, the music of the band, Diarrhea Planet, as well as the band's music videos and album art.

This poster was created in 2015 for Yale course Art 132 Intro Graphic Design, taught by Critic Yeju Choi. The typeface used is Good Dog.
